## Description

**Salah Companion** puts your daily prayer schedule right in your line of sight. Glance up and see all five prayers — Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha — with the next one highlighted and a live countdown, framed in a clean full-screen view alongside today's Hijri (Umm al-Qura) date.

Set it up once from the phone companion app: detect your location automatically, or search for any city in the world. Pick your calculation method and Asr madhab to match how your community observes. Your settings sync to the glasses and persist between sessions.

Prayer times are calculated on-device, so once your location is set the schedule keeps working — no need to pull out your phone to know when to pray.

### Key features

- 🕌 **All five daily prayers** on the glasses HUD, with the next prayer marked and a live "in 2h 14m" countdown
- 📅 **Today's Hijri date** (Umm al-Qura calendar) in the header
- 📍 **Automatic location** via your phone's GPS — accurate times wherever you are
- 🔎 **Search any city** worldwide from the companion app
- ⚙️ **12 calculation methods** — Muslim World League, ISNA, Umm al-Qura, Egyptian, Karachi, Dubai, Qatar, Kuwait, Singapore, Turkey, Tehran, Moonsighting
- 🕋 **Asr madhab** — Standard (Shafi) or Hanafi
- 💾 **Remembers your settings** across restarts
- 👓 **On-glasses controls** too — adjust city, method, and madhab from the touchpad or R1 ring

### How it works

1. Open **Salah Companion** in the Even Realities app.
2. Choose **Use my location** (GPS) or **Search for a city**.
3. Set your calculation method and Asr madhab.
4. Look up — your prayer times and countdown are on the glasses. The phone app is just for settings; the times live on the HUD.

### Permissions

- **Location** — to detect your current position for accurate prayer times. Optional: you can search for a city instead.
- **Network** — to look up cities by name (limited to the Open-Meteo geocoding service). City search is the only feature that uses the network.

No microphone, no camera, no account, no ads, no tracking.
